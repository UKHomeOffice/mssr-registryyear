For the 2023 modern slavery statement, your financial year-end date should fall between 1 July 2022 and 30 June 2023.

The registry categorises statements by the registry year they were due to be published rather than the financial year they cover.

You can calculate the registry year by adding 6 months to the date of your financial year-end date as the Home Office expects statements to be published within six months of the organisation’s financial year end.
