Upload PDF version of your statement

You can upload a PDF file of your full modern slavery statement here. It will be available publicly on the statement summary page once your statement is published.

The information in the PDF document should be similar to the information on the URL if you have added one.

{% set detailsHtml %}

You can create a PDF version of your modern slavery statement without buying specialised software.

  • Word processors (for example Microsoft Word or LibreOffice Writer) often provide the facility to directly save or export a document to PDF.
  • If your statement is only available on your website, Windows 10/11 and MacOS provide the ability to 'print' directly to PDF.

For further help, refer to the documentation for your word processing software / operating system, or contact your organisation's technical support.
