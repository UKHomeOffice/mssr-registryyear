You can calculate the registry year by adding 6 months to the date of your financial year-end as the Home Office expects statements to be published within six months of the organisation’s financial year-end.

{% set detailsHtml %}
  • If your financial year-end date falls in the first half of the calendar year (1 January to 30 June), submit your statement for the same calendar year.
    For example, if your financial year-end is 31 March 2022, the correct registry year would be 2022.
  • If your financial year-end date falls in the second half of the calendar year (1 July to 31 December), submit your statement in the next calendar year.
    For example, if your financial year-end is 31 December 2022, the correct registry year would be 2023.
